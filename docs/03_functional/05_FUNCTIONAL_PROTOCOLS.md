## What is a protocol?
A protocol defines a strategy of observation.

## Protocol types
- Baseline
- Focused
- Comparative
- Exploratory vs confirmatory

## Impact of protocol choices
- Segmentation affects temporal granularity
- Channel selection affects observable relationships
- Parameter choices affect resolution and stability

## What protocols do not do
- Do not interpret results
- Do not guarantee relevance
